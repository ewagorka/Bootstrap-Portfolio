About Me

Hello! My name is Ewa, and I am a 23-year-old with a passion for front-end development and UI/UX design. I recently signed up for a front-end development boot camp to further my knowledge of HTML, CSS, JavaScript, APIs, and React.👩‍💻

I am super excited to learn more about these technologies and use them to create stunning, functional websites that provide a great user experience. I'm very much looking forward to be able to turn my designs into real web pages and seeing them come to life.

Outside of coding, I love to travel and explore new places. I find that experiencing different cultures and ways of life helps to inspire my creativity and gives me new insights that I can apply to my work. I am also a huge cat and coffee lover 🐈.

Overall, I am excited and grateful to be a part of the Birmingham front-end development boot camp and can't wait to see where this journey takes me.
